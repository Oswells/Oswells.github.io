Sunrise by the Sea

  • Date：2020.10.12
  • Place：the Coast of Qinhuangdao
  • Fellow Traveler：Shuai Liu,ZhaoYang Liu,RuoYu Chen,etc.

On the early morning of December 12, after completing the application for postgraduate recommendation, we took a taxi to the seaside.

We saw the lights on the dock.

Saw a clear coastline in the clear moonlight.

We sat in the road, waiting for the sun to rise.

The sun was rising.

The sun had risen high and we went back to school.There is still a long way to go, and all the past is preface.
